# AgriSync — Consent Form

<!-- LEGAL_REVIEW_PENDING: Intro paragraph — English -->
We ask for three separate permissions to keep your data safe. Each permission is independent —
you can say yes to one and no to another. You can revoke at any time.

<!-- LEGAL_REVIEW_PENDING: Section 1 toggle — full history journal — English -->
## 1. Keep recordings of my voice beyond 30 days
- Default: OFF
- If you say yes: Your voice clips are saved with the full history, forever. You can review them any time.
- If you say no: They are automatically deleted after 30 days.

<!-- LEGAL_REVIEW_PENDING: Section 2 toggle — cross-farm aggregation — English -->
## 2. Improve the app using my de-identified data
- Default: OFF
- If you say yes: Your data (without your name) is used for learning — the app gets better for other farmers.

<!-- LEGAL_REVIEW_PENDING: Section 3 toggle — research corpus export — English -->
## 3. De-identified use for research / export
- Default: OFF
- If you say yes: General trends (without names) may be shared with government / research partners.

---

<!-- LEGAL_REVIEW_PENDING: Security + storage + transfer + rights block — English -->
**Security:** Your voice is encrypted on your phone (no one can read it even if stolen).
**Storage in India:** Your data is stored in India (Mumbai).
**Cross-border transfer notice:** Voice is sent to Google Gemini (US) for processing; only text comes back, nothing is stored there.
**Your rights:** View, edit, delete, download your data — in Settings.

<!-- LEGAL_REVIEW_PENDING: Revoke instruction line — English -->
To revoke: Settings → Consent → "Revoke All"
