# Phase 0 — Idea Validation (Paid Ads → Pain → Willingness to Pay)

> Goal: prove (a) people *feel* the pain we're naming, (b) they're curious enough to click, and (c) at least some are willing to put money down — *before* we build more.
> Method: small, structured paid-ad smoke test against multiple pain-point hypotheses; force a tiny payment as the truth-serum.

---

## 1. Strategic Principles (don't break these)
1. **Lead with pain, not product.** Never say "AgriSync is…" in the hook. Say "Tired of X?" / "Why does Y happen?"
2. **Curiosity gap > explanation.** The ad sells the *click*; the landing page sells the *signup*; the price sells the *truth*.
3. **Click ≠ validation. Pay = validation.** A ₹49 refundable hold is the only honest signal at this stage.
4. **Test pain points, not creatives.** We're not picking the best ad — we're picking the *sharpest pain*. Keep the format constant across pain variants so the variable is clean.
5. **Vernacular wins in agri.** Hindi/regional almost always beats English on cold cohorts. Budget reflects this.
6. **Show real, not rendered.** Phone-shot > studio. Real farmer faces > stock. Trust is the scarcest asset here.

---

## 2. Creative Hook Concepts (raw — to be down-selected)
*All hook lines are pain-first, curiosity-tipped. No product reveal in the hook.*

### Pain: Price Asymmetry / Mandi Opacity
- H1. "मंडी में ₹18 का टमाटर शहर में ₹60 — फरक कौन खा रहा है?"
- H2. "Your father got a better deal in 1992. Why?"
- H3. "The 3 numbers every farmer should see before sunrise. Most don't." → *swipe to find out*

### Pain: Delayed Payments / Cashflow Squeeze
- H4. "Buyer pays in 45 days. Dealer wants cash today. कुछ तो टूटेगा."
- H5. "If your khata had a heartbeat, it would be flatlining by the 20th of every month."

### Pain: Buyer / Demand Discovery
- H6. "There's a missing app between *your truck* and *their truck*." (15-sec reel: farmer sells low → buyer resells high)
- H7. "Stop asking 'भैया रेट क्या है?' Start knowing." (WhatsApp-screenshot styled ad)

### Pain: Input Trust (fake seeds / pesticide markup)
- H8. "You wouldn't buy medicine from a stranger. Why your seeds?"
- H9. "Same bag. Two villages. ₹400 difference. Why?"

### Pain: FPO / Coordination Chaos
- H10. "If you're an FPO secretary, you're using 4 WhatsApp groups, 2 notebooks, and your memory."
- H11. "Your FPO has 312 members. Your decisions reach ~40 of them. Math problem."

### Provocations (debate-bait, drive comments → algorithmic lift)
- H12. "Farmers don't have a price problem. They have an information problem."
- H13. "The middleman isn't the villain. The blind spot is."

### Wildcards
- H14. **"Anti-ad" ad** — black screen, white text only, no logo: *"This ad will be deleted in 7 days. So will the problem it's about — if 1,000 farmers click."*
- H15. **Founder selfie, no edits, 30 sec** — "Hi, I'm building AgriSync. I don't know if you'll pay for it. That's why this ad exists. Reserve a spot for ₹49 — if I can't earn it, I refund it." (Honesty-as-marketing; works on X/LinkedIn for early adopters & journos.)

## 3. Visual Treatments (pick 2 to start)
| Code | Treatment | Best for | Cost | Risk |
|------|-----------|----------|------|------|
| V-A | Gritty phone-shot, real farmer, vernacular voiceover | Cold rural cohort | Low | Needs real shoot |
| V-B | Side-by-side comparison frames (pain ↔ relief) | Meta feed/Shorts | Low | Generic if lazy |
| V-C | Animated infographic with single number anchored | Younger / urban-FPO / agritech-curious | Low | Less trust |
| V-D | Meme/agri-humor (in-language) | X, agri-Insta | Very low | Brand-dilution risk |
| V-E | "WhatsApp screenshot" static | Meta + WhatsApp ads | Very low | Hits hard if accurate |

**Recommendation for round 1:** V-A + V-E. Highest trust, fastest production.

---

## 4. Validation Funnel (the only one that matters)

```
Ad (curiosity) → 1-screen LP (clarity) → ₹49 reserve (commitment) → WhatsApp onboarding (qualitative)
```

- **LP rule:** one screen, one promise, one CTA. No nav, no logo wall, no "About us."
- **Why ₹49:** low enough nobody is *barred*, high enough nobody pays *casually*. Refundable / adjusted toward first month. This is the truth-serum.
- **WhatsApp follow-up:** every paid signup gets a 4-question voice-note interview. Real qual data, free.

---

## 5. Test Matrix (Round 1 — keep tiny)
- **Pain variants:** 3 (pick the 3 strongest hypotheses below)
- **Languages:** 2 (Hindi + 1 regional based on geo)
- **Formats:** 1 (single static OR single 15s reel — *not both* in round 1)
- **Geos:** 2 districts (one wedge geo, one control)
- **= ~12 ad sets max.** Resist the urge to expand.

**Budget posture:** ₹500–1,000 per ad set per day × 3 days = **~₹18k–36k total.** Anything less = noise. Anything more = wasted before we learn.

---

## 6. Decision Rules (what "validated" means)
| Stage | Metric | Pass bar (cold agri cohort) |
|-------|--------|------------------------------|
| Ad | CTR | **≥ 1.5%** |
| LP | LP-view → CTA click | **≥ 25%** |
| Pay | CTA click → ₹49 paid | **≥ 8–12%** |
| Cost | CAC per paid waitlist | **≤ ₹150** (geo-dependent) |
| Wedge signal | Best pain ÷ worst pain on paid-CVR | **≥ 2×** |

**If wedge signal is < 2×:** we don't have a pain hierarchy yet — rewrite hooks, don't scale spend.
**If pay-rate is < 5% across the board:** the pain is real but our *promise* is weak (or the ₹49 is a wrong proxy). Iterate the LP, not the ad.
**If CTR is great but pay-rate dies:** classic curiosity-without-substance. We're being clever, not useful. Kill that hook.

---

## 7. Things We Are *Not* Testing in Phase 0
- Brand identity / logo / colors (Phase 1)
- Influencers (Phase 1)
- PR (Phase 2 — needs traction story first)
- Pricing tiers (Phase 1)
- Channel mix beyond Meta + WhatsApp (Phase 1)

Discipline here is the whole point.

---

## 8. Open Questions (block us from sharpening)
1. **Primary user:** farmer, FPO, dealer, buyer? *(determines pain order)*
2. **Wedge geography:** which 1–2 districts/states? *(determines language + crop context)*
3. **Strongest pain hypothesis** *(your gut, before data)*: price discovery / buyer discovery / payments / inputs / coordination?
4. **Founder on camera — yes/no?** *(unlocks H15 + V-A authenticity)*
5. **Budget ceiling for round 1?** *(shapes matrix size)*
